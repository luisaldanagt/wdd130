Guatemala

Guatemala is a country in Central America known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and ancient history. It is home to impressive Mayan ruins, colorful markets, and vibrant traditions that blend Indigenous and Spanish influences. From lush rainforests and volcanoes to beautiful lakes like Lake Atitlán, Guatemala offers a unique mix of natural beauty and historical significance.
